Nauryz

The Kazakh Student Association (KSA) has a cherished history of commemorating Nauryz, the Kazakh New Year, through vibrant and engaging events. These celebrations have brought the heartwarming traditions of Kazakhstan to life, featuring captivating displays of music, dance, and cultural performances. Through interactive fairs and workshops, KSA has provided a platform for attendees to indulge in the rich tapestry of Kazakh heritage, from savoring traditional culinary delights to immersing themselves in the country's crafts and art forms. These Nauryz events have not only celebrated Kazakhstan's culture but have also united diverse communities, fostering understanding, connection, and a sense of belonging among students at UBC. As we move forward, KSA eagerly anticipates organizing more memorable Nauryz festivities that continue to bridge cultures and create cherished memories for all.
